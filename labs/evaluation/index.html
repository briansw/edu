Evaluation Criteria

Communication

  • Is the message being communicated clearly?
  • Where did you become confused?
  • How might it be refined?

Navigation and interactivity

  • Does the project take advantage of the medium?
  • Is it usable? Are the interactive elements recognizable and easy to use?
  • Does interacting with the content shape my experience? Am I passively reading/observing?
  • Can I easily understand the structure of the navigation or do I get lost in the experience?

Visual form

  • Is it visually interesting?
  • Does the form make sense for the content?
  • How might it be refined?

Execution and craftsmanship

  • Does it work?
  • Does it look intentional? Or am I seeing half the content running off the side of the page?
  • Were various states considered appropriately?
